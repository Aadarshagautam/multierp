Shop Software Starter Guide

Product:
CommerceOS Shop Software

What this software is for:
- Retail stores
- Mini marts
- General shops
- Multi-branch retail operations

Core modules:
- Retail POS
- Customer ledger and credit sales
- Inventory and stock room
- Invoices and receipts
- Accounting suite
- Sales and branch reports

Staff roles:
- Manager: branch stock, sales, discount control
- Accountant: journals, receivables, payables, tax
- Cashier: item billing, payment collection, daily closing

Branch support:
- Separate branch users and access rules
- Branch-level stock and finance
- Central owner reporting

How to start:
1. Create your account.
2. Create your first shop branch.
3. Add products, tax, pricing, and stock.
4. Assign manager, accountant, and cashier roles.
5. Start billing and tracking inventory.

Use the web version:
Open the app and sign in from the CommerceOS login page.
