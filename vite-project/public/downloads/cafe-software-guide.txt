Cafe Software Starter Guide

Product:
CommerceOS Cafe Software

What this software is for:
- Coffee shops
- Cafes
- Bakeries
- Fast counter service businesses

Core modules:
- Fast POS billing
- Customer loyalty
- Customer management
- Stock alerts
- Shift and register control
- Accounting and sales reports

Staff roles:
- Manager: pricing, branch overview, repeat-customer review
- Accountant: cash summary, supplier bills, reporting
- Cashier: fast billing, loyalty points, shift handling

Branch support:
- One owner account for many cafe branches
- Separate cashier, manager, and accountant by branch
- Central branch comparison

How to start:
1. Create your account.
2. Create your cafe branch.
3. Add products, combos, taxes, and payment methods.
4. Set loyalty rules and assign staff roles.
5. Start billing from the POS screen.

Use the web version:
Open the app and sign in from the CommerceOS login page.
