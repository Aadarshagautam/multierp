Restaurant Software Starter Guide

Product:
CommerceOS Restaurant Software

What this software is for:
- Dine-in restaurants
- Food courts
- Delivery kitchens
- Multi-branch restaurant groups

Core modules:
- Table and floor plan
- Kitchen display
- POS billing
- CRM and loyalty
- Inventory and wastage control
- Accounting and reports

Staff roles:
- Manager: branch sales, tables, kitchen, pricing, reports
- Accountant: purchases, journals, tax, profit and loss
- Cashier: billing, receipts, shift open and close

Branch support:
- Separate branch users
- Separate branch reporting
- Central owner dashboard

How to start:
1. Create your account.
2. Create your first restaurant branch.
3. Add tables, menu items, taxes, and pricing.
4. Assign manager, accountant, and cashier roles.
5. Start billing from the POS screen.

Use the web version:
Open the app and sign in from the CommerceOS login page.
